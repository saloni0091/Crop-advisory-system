WHAT WE DO

In order to mitigate the agrarian crisis in the current scenario, there is a need for better recommendation systems to alleviate the crisis by helping the farmers to take an informed decision before starting the cultivation of crops.

We will built a portal for the Farmers which would help them to get assistance regarding crop cultivation strategies, primarily for the services of: prediction, recommendation, information of crops. Importantly To recommend optimum crops to be cultivated by farmers based on several parameters and help them make an informed decision before cultivation.

Farmer being the main pillar of our Economy, it's our responsibility to carry out the best practices for the upliftment of both
nation and farmers.

 MISSION: Our mission is to empower all the farmers of our nation making them capable enough to take the best decisions for their life.

VISION: We are having the vision that in the near upcoming future our service be made available to each and every farmer.
